## Dash-free training version

The "dash-free" version of inbound bodies preserves content but removes em/en dashes. Used for training inputs where dashes could be confused with minus signs.

## What the agent should learn

- Humans respond in SHORT bursts (< 50 chars dominant in this corpus: 92%).
- Reply style is direct and lowercase-leaning — no greeting ceremony.
- Common openers: see top 10 above; varies widely across the corpus.

## What the agent should avoid

- Don't over-explain to humans. They expect terse replies.
- Don't add "Hi [name]!" preambles in reply contexts — humans didn't.
- Don't assume politeness markers like "please" — most inbound bodies don't include them.
